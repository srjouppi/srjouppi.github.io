The Cost of COVID

An analysis of 3,500+ GoFundMe's created by and for people who have suffered from COVID.

I was looking through a hospital's list of standard charges when I saw a whole section dedicated to uninsured COVID patients. And one charge took my breath away.

The cost of more than 4 days on a ventilator: $120,000.

This made me wonder, how much was all of this costing those who become hospitalized with COVID? Using BeautifulSoup and Selenium I scraped over 5,000 campaigns from GoFundMe and narrowed that selection down to 3,500 that I am pretty confident are related to people who have been diagnosed with COVID.

The average campaign was $15,500, and the average campaign specifically related to medical bills was $19,000

Using regex, I found 159 mentions of days, months or weeks spent in the hospital. 18% of those had hospital stays greater than two months. The average stay was 43 days.
