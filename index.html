Visualizing the child mental health care gap

There are nearly 1.3 million children who rely on Medicaid for health coverage in New York City. I wanted to know, how many child psychologists are accessible to them?

I created a scraper to cross reference Medicaid provider codes with the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services' NPI Provider Lookup tool to figure out who specialized in treating children.

I visualized the results with a combination of handmade illustrations, ai2html and d3 to create this scrollytelling.

🏭 Creating a living dataset of Michigan air pollution records

For my final Data & Databases project, I scraped 18,000+ pdfs from the Michigan Department of Environment, Great Lakes and Energy to create a searchable dataset of air pollution records by type of document (i.e. violation notice, compliance evaluation, stack test report), date, company, county, zip code and address.

🔍 My auto-scraper searches the Michigan database daily for new documents and adds them to existing datasets.

📍 I created an interactive map of total violations by zip code and included basic demographic information from the Census Bureau.
